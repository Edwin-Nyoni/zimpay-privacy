Privacy Policy

ZimPay+ v1.5.1 — Your data stays on your device. Always.

Short version: ZimPay+ processes your mobile money messages only on your device. We never collect, store, or share your data with anyone. Everything stays on your phone.

1. Introduction

ZimPay+ ("the App") is a personal finance tool developed by ZimPay+, based in Zimbabwe, that helps you track your mobile money transactions automatically. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy — by design, not just by policy.

2. Information We Access

The App requests access to the following on your device:

  • SMS Messages — The app reads SMS message metadata and text locally, solely to identify mobile money transaction confirmations from EcoCash (+263164USD) and Econet Bundles (+263143). No other SMS messages are accessed or stored.
  • Notifications — to send you spending alerts and summaries that you configure within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • SMS data is read and processed entirely on your device.
  • Transaction data is used only to display your history, balances, and analytics within the App.
  • Exported reports (PDF/Excel) are generated on your device and shared only when you choose to share them.
  • Notifications are generated locally based on thresholds you set.

4. Data Storage

ZimPay+ does not use any servers, cloud storage, or databases. All data is processed in memory and never written to permanent storage outside your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your personal data to any third party. Data is not shared with third parties unless the user explicitly chooses to export or consent to external services. Your messages and transaction data never leave your device unless you explicitly export and share a report yourself.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in ZimPay+.

7. Permissions Explained

  • READ_SMS — Required to read mobile money confirmation messages. Without this, the App cannot function.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Required to deliver spending alerts you configure. You can disable this at any time in your device settings.
  • VIBRATE — Used for notification feedback only.

8. Children's Privacy

ZimPay+ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Security

Since all data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted, your information is protected by your device's own security (PIN, fingerprint, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure and up to date.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Data Retention

ZimPay+ does not retain any data beyond your current session. All transaction data is read from your device's SMS inbox each time you open the App and is never written to any external or persistent storage. Deleting the App removes all locally cached data.

12. Your Rights

Since ZimPay+ does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no data to request, correct, or delete on our end. You are in full control of your data at all times. You may revoke SMS or notification permissions at any time through your device settings, which will limit the App's functionality.
