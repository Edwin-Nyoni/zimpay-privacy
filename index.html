Privacy Policy

Short version: ZimPay Tracker reads your EcoCash SMS messages only on your device. We never collect, store, or share your data with anyone. Everything stays on your phone.

1. Introduction

ZimPay Tracker ("the App") is a personal finance tool that helps you track your EcoCash mobile money transactions. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Access

The App requests access to the following on your device:

  • SMS Messages – to read EcoCash transaction confirmation messages so we can display your transaction history, balance, and spending summaries.
  • Notifications – to send you spending alerts and summaries that you configure within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • SMS data is read and processed entirely on your device.
  • Transaction data is used only to display your history, balances, and analytics within the App.
  • Exported reports (PDF/Excel) are generated on your device and shared only when you choose to share them.
  • Notifications are generated locally based on thresholds you set.

4. Data Storage

ZimPay Tracker does not use any servers, cloud storage, or databases. All data is processed in memory and never written to permanent storage outside your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your personal data to any third party. Your SMS messages and transaction data never leave your device unless you explicitly export and share a report yourself.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in ZimPay Tracker.

7. Permissions Explained

  • READ_SMS – Required to read EcoCash confirmation messages. Without this, the App cannot function.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Required to deliver spending alerts you configure. You can disable this at any time in your device settings.
  • VIBRATE – Used for notification feedback only.

8. Children's Privacy

ZimPay Tracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Security

Since all data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted, your information is protected by your device's own security (PIN, fingerprint, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure and up to date.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact us at: zimpayplus@gmail.com
